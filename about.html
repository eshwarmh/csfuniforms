About CSF Uniforms

Learn more about our company and values

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Color Shine Fashions (CSF Uniforms) began as a small uniform manufacturing unit in Bangalore. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted name in the uniform industry, serving schools, hospitals, and corporate clients across the region.

Our Vision

To be the preferred uniform supplier by delivering high-quality products with exceptional customer service, while maintaining ethical business practices and contributing positively to our community.

Why Choose Us?

  • Premium quality fabrics and materials
  • Custom designs tailored to your needs
  • Competitive pricing without compromising quality
  • Reliable and timely delivery
  • Local manufacturing in Bangalore

Our Core Values

Quality
